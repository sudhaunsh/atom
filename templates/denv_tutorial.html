How to use DenV Online


  • Enter the name of the patient in the Name field.
  • Enter the age of the patient in the Age field. 20 years => 20
  • Enter the date of onset of the disease in the Date of onset field. 3rd May 2004 => 3/5/2004
  • Enter the DAY of the first test in the Date of first test field. 4th May 2004 => 1
  • Enter the count of the first test in the Count of first test field. 250 thousand => 250
  • Enter the date of the second test in the Date of second test field. 6th May 2004 => 3
  • Enter the count of the second test in the Count of second test field. 200 thousand => 200
  • Click on the Submit button.
  • The result will be displayed on the next page.
